# Official NMSC guide snapshots

This directory archives annual editions of NMSC's *Guide to the National Merit
Scholarship Program*. The Virginia rows are in the guide's
"Semifinalists and Commended Students" table on printed pages 13-14 (PDF pages
17-18). The `Schools` value is NMSC's number of schools represented by the
Semifinalists assigned to that selection unit.

The Virginia values in these guides are **state-selection-unit totals**, not
counts of every Semifinalist attending a school physically located in Virginia.
NMSC treats qualifying U.S. boarding schools as separate regional selection
units; the table's footnote says those schools enroll a substantial portion of
students from outside their state. By contrast, the Virginia media-list packets
used elsewhere in this project are organized by city and high school and include
students at Virginia-located boarding schools. The two scopes therefore need not
match and must not be substituted for one another.

NMSC publishes the current edition at its reusable official guide URL:
<https://www.nationalmerit.org/s/1758/images/gid2/editor_documents/guide_to_the_national_merit_scholarship_program.pdf>.
Because that URL changes content as a new edition is published, the local
filename, issue month, and SHA-256 above identify each archived snapshot.

These guides support selection-unit totals and cross-checks only. They do not
contain the school-by-school named Virginia list, cannot create school-level
observations, and cannot justify `verified_zero` for a school absent from another
source. Any statewide-share calculation must align its numerator with either the
NMSC selection-unit scope or the Virginia-school-location scope explicitly.

The statistical table itself carries an NMSC proprietary-information notice.
Keep use of these archived documents limited to source verification and minimal
aggregate citation; do not republish the complete table.
