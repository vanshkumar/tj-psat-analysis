# TJHSST Class 2025 Admissions By Source School

`tjhsst_class_2025_by_middle_school.xlsx` is an unchanged public mirror of an
FCPS-origin FOIA response for the TJHSST Class of 2025.

- Public mirror: <https://www.fcag.org/2025%20by%20Middle%20School.xlsx>
- Retrieved: 2026-07-17
- Workbook created: 2021-07-06
- Workbook modified: 2021-07-20
- SHA-256: `948c91238520afab9ffe21af28d60b6c65fe11f5533428cd692b7f6698566911`
- Sheet: `2025 by Middle School`

The workbook package names Jeremy A. Shughart as creator and Molly Shannon as
last modifier. Its internal absolute-path metadata points to the FCPS OCCR FOIA
Team SharePoint folder `TJHSST Data_Responses/2025 by Middle School`, supporting
FCPS provenance even though FCAG hosts the public copy.

The source contains no student names. It replaces cells representing 10 or
fewer students with `TS` under its FERPA disclosure rule. Processed outputs keep
those cells blank with an explicit suppression status; they never treat `TS` as
zero, ten, or an imputed midpoint.
